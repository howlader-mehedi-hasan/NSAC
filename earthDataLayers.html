Google Earth View: South Pacific Flyover

Imagery (c) OpenStreetMap contributors

This globe recreates the Google Earth vantage point you shared (lat -3.834 deg, lon -142.989 deg) from roughly 19,900 km above the South Pacific. Rotate or zoom to explore the surrounding ocean and equatorial Pacific islands.

  • Imagery sourced from open OpenStreetMap tiles, rendered on a 3D globe.
  • Camera heading and tilt are matched to the 207 deg / 60 deg view from your Google Maps link.
  • Orange marker pins the exact coordinate so you can recenter after moving around.

Tip: Shift+drag for tilt, scroll to zoom. Use the reset button below if you want to snap back to the shared viewpoint.
